New Puppy Checklist

Puppy Care 101

🍽️

Feeding

Feed 3x daily for puppies under 6 months. Use age-appropriate food and measure portions carefully to ensure proper growth.

🏃

Exercise

5 minutes per month of age, twice daily. Avoid over-exercising growing joints to prevent long-term health issues.

🏥

Vet Care

First vet visit within 48 hours. Keep up with vaccinations and monthly preventatives for fleas and worms.

😴

Sleep

Puppies need 16–18 hours of sleep daily. A crate helps establish healthy sleep routines and provides a safe space.
